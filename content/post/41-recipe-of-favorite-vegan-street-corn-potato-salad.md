##### The ingredients needed to make Vegan Street Corn Potato Salad:

1. Prepare 2 cans corn

1. Take 1/2 cup chopped cilantro
1. Make ready 2 tablespoons chipotle spice
1. Take 1 tablespoon Tajin spice
